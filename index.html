HOW IT WORKS





Learn for free

Simply download Khawna on your smartphone and learn on a variety of subjects—even while on-the-go—at your own pace and on your own time.





Earn Points

Collect points as you complete a course through Khawna. The more classes you take, the more points you earn.





Get Rewards

Use your earned points to avail paid courses and redeem prizes from Khawna’s rewards.
